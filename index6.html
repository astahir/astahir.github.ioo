Other Opportunities

As more and more families across America are affected by Alzheimer's, many teenagers are becoming aware of this disease. In an effort to provide another source of opportunity, we are providing a scholarship to the student with the best autobiography. This scholarship allows students to critically think about their personal story with Alzheimer's disease.

Eligibility

  • Plan to enter a college/university within 12 months of the scholarship deadline
  • Currently enrolled in a public high school in the US (must provide documentation of registration)
  • Submit all materials by February 25, 2017

Requirements

  1. Complete and submit online application
  2. 1200 to 1500 essay
    3. -How has Alzheimer's changed or impacted your life?
    -What have you learned about yourself, family, or community while coping with Alzheimer's?
  3. Current High-School Transcript
  4. 200 Word Autobiography (Resumes not accepted)
