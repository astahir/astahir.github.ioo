Donations

Every dollar you raise benefits those affected by Alzheimer's disease in your community.
The Alzheimer's Awareness Club works diligently for advance care, support, and research across the world. The money your raise makes a difference in the lives of those facing Alzheimer's disease.

The Alzheimer's Association:

Provides care and support to all those facing Alzheimer's

  • In-person support groups, connecting those facing with the disease
  • A free nationwide 24/7 Helpline: HelpLine
  • Online message boards through: help@alzheimers.net

Three reasons why you should help raise awareness

  1. When awareness is created, it helps to create more treatment options for those who have the condition, and also helps create more ways to prevent different forms of dementia.
  2. When the signs are recognized early, detection can occur, and treatment can be provided early on.
  3. Awareness also helps remind people of the importance of caregivers and health providers, who play a large role in helping those with Alzheimer's carry on through life.

The researchers from John Hopkins are conducting numerous studies including anti-amyloid therapy for Alzheimer's disease. They are also researching the role of other brain chemicals in the formation of Alzheimer's disease.
